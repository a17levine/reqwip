About

reQwip makes it easy to buy and sell new and used sports gear locally! Inventory is listed by individuals and shops. Purchases are made with a credit card for security. Once you make a purchase, just pick up your item and you’re good to go!

  • SELL YOUR GEAR FOR CASH!
  • BUY GEAR FROM PEERS AND SHOPS
  • LOCAL PICKUPS ONLY, NO SHIPPING
  • NO ANONYMOUS USERS
